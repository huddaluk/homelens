Our Home Visit Services

HomeLens provides comprehensive property inspections at your location, giving you the peace of mind you need when making important property decisions.

What's Included in Our Inspections

  • Structural Assessment: Visual examination of walls, floors, ceilings, and foundations
  • Roof Inspection: Exterior roof condition and accessible roof spaces
  • Electrical Systems: Visual check of electrical panels, outlets, and fixtures
  • Plumbing Systems: Inspection of pipes, fixtures, and water systems
  • Heating & Ventilation: Assessment of heating systems and ventilation
  • Windows & Doors: Condition and functionality evaluation

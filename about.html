About HomeLens

Professional property inspection services across Wigan

Who We Are

HomeLens is a leading property inspection company based in Wigan, providing comprehensive visual assessments for homeowners, buyers, and sellers. Our team of ACABE-accredited inspectors delivers professional, reliable, and detailed property reports.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional property inspection services that give our clients confidence in their property decisions. We believe in thorough assessments, clear communication, and professional excellence in everything we do.

Why Choose HomeLens?

  • ACABE Accredited: Our inspectors are certified by the Chartered Association of Building Engineers
  • Local Expertise: Deep knowledge of properties across Wigan and surrounding areas
  • Professional Standards: Comprehensive reports with actionable insights
  • Fast Turnaround: Quick scheduling and rapid report delivery
  • Competitive Pricing: Professional services at fair, transparent rates

Our Services

We offer a range of property inspection services including:

  • Comprehensive visual property assessments
  • Detailed inspection reports with photos
  • Online consultations and document reviews
  • Home visit inspections
  • Professional property advice

Our Commitment

At HomeLens, we're committed to providing the highest quality property inspection services. We understand that buying, selling, or maintaining a property is one of the most significant decisions you'll make, and we're here to ensure you have all the information you need to make informed choices.
